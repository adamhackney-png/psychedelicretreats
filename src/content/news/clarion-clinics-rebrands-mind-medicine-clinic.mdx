Clarion Clinics, a purpose-built psychedelic-assisted therapy clinic in Abbotsford, Melbourne, now operates as the **Mind Medicine Australia Clinic and Centre of Excellence**, a joint initiative of the charity Mind Medicine Australia and clinical operator Psychennex. The old clarionclinics.com domain now redirects to the clinic's new site.

## What we know

The clinic — described as Australia's first purpose-built psychedelic-assisted therapy clinic, with seven treatment rooms plus a group room — continues offering clinic-based MDMA (for PTSD) and psilocybin (for treatment-resistant depression) therapy under the TGA Authorised Prescriber scheme, delivered by individually authorised psychiatrists.
