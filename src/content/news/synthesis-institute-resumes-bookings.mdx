Synthesis Institute, a Netherlands-based legal psilocybin-truffle retreat operator, underwent a publicly reported financial collapse in March 2023. As of our research for this directory, it appears to have resumed operating, with 2026 retreat dates live on its booking system.

## Background

The 2023 collapse was reported by TechCrunch at the time. We have not independently confirmed the current corporate entity, ownership structure, or financial standing behind the retreats currently being booked under the Synthesis Institute name.
