## What to expect

Most legal Netherlands retreats run 3–6 day residential programs at rural retreat centres within roughly 1–2 hours of Amsterdam, built around one or two guided group truffle ceremonies with preparation and integration support before and after. Operators vary in group size (roughly 9–20 participants across the operators we've reviewed), facilitator ratio, and how much medical/psychological screening they require before accepting a booking — see each listing's vetting checklist rather than assuming a standard.

## Logistics from Australia

Direct flights from Australia to Amsterdam don't currently exist for most carriers — expect one or two stops (commonly via the Middle East or Asia) and a total travel time in the order of 22–30 hours each way. Time zone difference is significant (the Netherlands is roughly 8–9 hours behind Australian Eastern time, varying with daylight saving on both ends), so most retreats build in an arrival buffer day, which this page's cost table includes as extra accommodation nights.
