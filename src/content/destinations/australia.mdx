## What to expect

Access to clinical psychedelic-assisted therapy in Australia is narrow by design: only a small number of TGA Authorised Prescribers exist nationally, treatment is a multi-month structured protocol (not a single session), and it's reserved for the two approved conditions. Ketamine treatment for depression is more widely available, including through some public hospital services, but is still referral-only and not a walk-in service. See our clinics directory for factually-listed, neutral-mode services in this category — we don't display pricing or promotional content for these listings, in line with TGA advertising rules for prescription-only medicines.

## Domestic logistics

Most Australian clinical services are concentrated in capital cities (particularly Melbourne and Sydney), so regional and rural patients may need to travel or rely on the increasing use of telehealth for the psychotherapy components of treatment. Costs and Medicare/funding assistance vary by clinic and by patient circumstances — always confirm directly with the specific service.
