## What to expect

Retreats we've reviewed near Iquitos run 7–12 day residential programs, combining traditional Shipibo-Conibo-led ceremonies with Western, trauma-informed facilitation at some centres. Screening rigor, group size, and published emergency protocols vary substantially between operators — this is one of the most important categories to read the vetting checklist for, not just the marketing copy.

## Logistics from Australia

Expect a long routing — commonly via the US or Chile to Lima, then a domestic flight to Iquitos (Peru's Amazon region isn't reachable by road from Lima) — with total travel time well over 30 hours door-to-door including layovers and the domestic leg. Altitude isn't a major factor for Iquitos itself (it's lowland Amazon, not the Andes), but if your itinerary includes Cusco or other highland stops, altitude acclimatisation is a separate consideration.
