## Logistics from Australia

There are no direct flights from Australia to Jamaica — expect a long routing, commonly via the US or Canada, with total travel time well over 24 hours each way including layovers. Note that a US or Canadian transit/connection may carry its own legal considerations around psilocybin possession, even though you would not be carrying anything illegal on the outbound leg — verify your specific routing and any transit-country rules before booking.
