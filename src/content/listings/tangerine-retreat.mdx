Tangerine Retreat (operated by Tangerine Institute BV) runs 3-day and 5-day residential psilocybin-truffle retreats near Loenen, about 90 minutes from Amsterdam, for groups of roughly 9–12 participants with a stated coach ratio of around 1:3.

The operator describes itself as a fully legal organisation operating under Dutch truffle law, requires a pre-approval medical questionnaire (minimum age 25, contraindications reviewed), and states that a GP or nurse is present during ceremonies as a named safety feature, with facilitators available day and night. The site cites a 5.0 rating from 27 Google reviews.
