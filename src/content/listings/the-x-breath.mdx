The X Breath runs substance-free, small-group retreats in the Byron Bay region combining Conscious Connected Breathwork with Qi Gong, yoga, and nature-based practices, led by Mark Moon with co-facilitator Em Cruickshank on one-day sessions. A signature multi-day "Breath of Life" retreat is also offered.

The operator publishes a breathwork waiver document for participants.
