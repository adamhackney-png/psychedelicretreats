The School of Breathwork runs a substance-free, 6-day residential group breathwork immersion at Gymea Retreat near Uki, in the Byron Bay hinterland, founded and led by Eleanor Mann with a team of breathwork practitioners. Some sessions count toward the school's own Breathwork Practitioner Training certification. The school also runs retreats in Spain and Portugal.
