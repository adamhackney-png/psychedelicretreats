Beckley Retreats' Jamaica program runs the same eleven-week structure as its Netherlands program — around four weeks of virtual preparation, a 5–6 day in-person retreat, and roughly six weeks of integration support — held at Good Hope Estate, about 45 minutes from Montego Bay's airport.

Psilocybin mushrooms were never scheduled under Jamaica's Dangerous Drugs Act, and Beckley's own materials state this explicitly. Facilitator bios published by the program include Head of Research Dr AZA Allsop, and the program describes a similar ~1:3 facilitator ratio to its other locations.

Published pricing is roughly USD $5,975–$11,550 (approx. <AudPrice usd={5975} usdHigh={11550} />) depending on room type and occupancy.
