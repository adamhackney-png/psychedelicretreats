Dr Benjamin Stiel (DPsych, MPhil, BSc(Hons), Dip Ed, BA) offers one-on-one talk therapy from Redfern, Sydney, and via Zoom telehealth, specifically for people integrating psychedelic experiences they undertook independently. This is integration support only — not a dosing, facilitation, or supply service. Published rates are AUD $300 (75 minutes) and $340 (90 minutes), with a Medicare rebate of $141.85 available with a GP Mental Health Care Plan.
