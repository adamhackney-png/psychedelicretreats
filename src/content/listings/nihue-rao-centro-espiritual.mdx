Nihue Rao Centro Espiritual offers ayahuasca retreats of a minimum 7 days (four ceremonies), with longer stays of 2–4 weeks recommended by the centre for deeper work, plus a separate multi-month "School of Ancestral Knowledge" shamanism apprenticeship track. Ceremonies are led by Maestro Ricardo Amaringo, a Shipibo-Conibo shaman the site describes as having over 44 years of experience and having trained more than 30 apprentices.

The centre publishes a dedicated reviews page on its own site, separate from third-party platforms.
