Synthesis Institute's immersion retreat is a 5-day in-person program set inside a roughly seven-week arc (about three weeks of preparation calls, the retreat, and three weeks of integration support), held at a property in the Hemrik area, around 90 minutes from Amsterdam.

The program describes a detailed three-step screening process — application, a refundable deposit, and a comprehensive health screening — with an explicit contraindication list covering psychosis, suicidal ideation, dissociative disorders, personality disorders, and prior psychiatric hospitalisation. Group size is stated as up to 15–20 participants with a roughly 1:3–4 facilitator ratio.
